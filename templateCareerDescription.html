We’re looking for a highly-driven HR & Admin Officer to join our ever-expanding team in Makati. This is a great chance for you to be part of a fun and fast-paced multinational environment and enjoy a number of benefits and perks which include, but are not limited to, medical and dental plans, training allowances, free food, and monthly activities that bring employees together.

Top Tech Talent is a fast-growing UK-managed offshore recruitment and staffing company in the Philippines. As we tackle the growing global IT skills shortage, we strive to create a professional workplace that empowers its staff to reach their full potential. We provide clear career paths for our staff along with individual training programs and excellent reward and bonus systems.

The Position

The HR & Admin Officer administers the organisation’s benefits, policies, and procedures. This role also provides general support and assistance on the administration of the day-to-day operations/activities of the human resources and admin departments.

Reporting to the Assistant HR Manager and the Country Manager, the successful HR & Admin Officer will be tasked to:

  • Prepare documents including, but not limited to, job offers, contract employment and contractor's agreement for upcoming employees as requested by recruitment.
  • Route the contracts and other documents for signature to the respective departments.
  • Make sure that all job descriptions are provided and kept in the HR Google document.
  • Update all trackers such as New Joiner's Sheet and Manila EID.
  • Manage the pre-employment and ensure that all had compiled with the 2-week period of submission.
  • Check and review the information of the new employee records in HRWorkz.
  • Ensure that the release of the company ID is done in a timely manner.
  • Ensure that all 201 filing is being done by the HR assistant in a timely manner.
  • Perform contract monitoring for Contractors agreement renewal and those that are due for regularisation.
  • Send out email reminders to all department concerns for those that are due of renewal, regularisation, and annual appraisals.
  • Prepare and send out appraisal forms to respective Department Heads.
  • Prepare Performance Improvement Plan(PIP) as recommended by the Department Heads.
  • Monitor issued PIP and coordinate with various Department Heads.
Candidate Requirements

The HR & Admin Officer administers the organisation’s benefits, policies, and procedures. This role also provides general support and assistance on the administration of the day-to-day operations/activities of the human resources and admin departments.

Reporting to the Assistant HR Manager and the Country Manager, the successful HR & Admin Officer will be tasked to:

  • A Bachelor's/College Degree or Post-Graduate Diploma/Master's Degree in Human Resources, Psychology Administration, or a related field.
  • At least 3 years of work experience in a similar capacity/role, preferably with similar organisations.
  • Adept in Philippine Labour Laws.
  • Knowledgeable in standard software applications including MS Windows, MS Office(Word, Excel, PowerPoint), and other office productivity tools is advantage/Excellent oral and written English communication skills.
  • Self-starter, highly motivated, goal-oriented, persistent, fast learner, team player, emphatic, confident, possess good interpersonal skills, maintain strict confidentially, mature, can deal with different types of personalities, and has the ability to multitask.
