Why hire software software developers in the Philippines

The Philippines, specifically Manila, is one of the world's top outsourcing hubs

Tholons, an international advisory firm for global outsourcing, has consistently cited Manila, Philippines as one among the top outsourcing destinations. Worldwide recognition of the Philippines as an outsourcing hub goes back close to a decade ago when the UK's National Outsourcing Association proclaimed the country as the world's best BPO destination three times.

164,600 IT and Tech graduates every year

The Philippines continues to produce more and more graduates yearly. The Philippines' Commission on Higher Education (CHED), there are approximately 160,000+ graduates in engineering, tech, and IT programs every year from internationally recognised universities.

High English Proficiency

The Philippines is among the top 5 largest English speaking countries where over 90% of the population speaks and understands English. The country also scored high on the EF English Proficiency Index (6th Edition) ranking 13th out of 72 countries, just behind Malaysia and even ahead of Switzerland.
