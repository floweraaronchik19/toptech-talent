The world we believe in

Outsourcing is the past, Top Tech Talent is innovating the future of global tech teams.

We believe in a world where good talents get matched with the right opportunities, anywhere in the world. Talent is the world's most precious resource - and it can be found even in the most remote parts of the world. Finding the right talent to execute on innovative ideas is the bedrock of Top Tech Talent. We believe that by bridging the gap across borders, we enable businesses to scale, we create opportunities for workers to grow, and we make the world a little bit closer.

We believe in a world where globally sourced talent can be both cost effective and productive. As we help companies save over 70% on their developer cost, we enable companies with great ideas to get off the ground. As we build out a platform for companies to lead strong global tech teams, we enable the best in the world to work on great ideas. As we prove the success of our business model time and time again, we break down the stigma of "outsourcing," one Top Tech Talent at a time.

The purpose we serve

We give companies access to a global talent pool of only the top developers.

After hire, we also provide all the support and infrastructure to make your employee successful throughout the entire term. We do all the backend work, so you can focus on growing your business.

Here are some of our client's favorite features about Top Tech Talent:

The highest quality of tech talent on the market
Full client control, as candidates are approved for the job role
Silicon Valley style offices with an entrepreneurial culture
Dedicated teams for support and operations
Managed IT services
Multi-cultural management team actively breaking down cultural barriers
24/7 support as your on the ground representatives

In the current status quo of outsourcing, we often see businesses struggle to hire the right talent. On the flip side, we often see traditionally outsourced developers struggle to be treated as an equal part of the team. We look to break that status quo. We believe that the IT market should be global, not local, with every company getting the chance to hire the best person for their needs. We believe that a global hire should be integrated with the core team, otherwise, how can we expect someone to do their best work if they are not given the opportunity? Top Tech Talent is built on the foundation of bridging the gap. Giving the capacity for companies to hire globally while at the same time ensuring the employees are treated as an in-house hire. We assimilate the employee as an equal part of the client's company, building pride and belief in the company they represent.

The future we build

Top Tech Talent Global Teams = IT Recruiting + CoWorking Spaces + Managed Services

The future of outsourcing is having access to global talent with supported infrastructure. Our office spaces are modeled after our client company's design; the culture we instill in our developers mirrors the client company's values. That's how outsourcing should be done: personalized, dedicated, committed.
