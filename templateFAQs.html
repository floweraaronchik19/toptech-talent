Who We Are?
What does Top Tech Talent do?
Top Tech Talent Ltd is a UK registered company that specialises in supplying Western businesses with dedicated Web, Mobile and Software Developers who work from our managed facilities in the Philippines. We have offices in London, UK, and in Manila and Cebu City in the Philippines. Our British founders have several years of experience in IT Recruitment, Internet Startups, Outsourcing and working in the Philippines. We have a database of over 100,000 professionals in the Philippines. Our process is to headhunt and pre-assess candidates for you to choose from. Once you have reviewed the CVs rates, your Account Manager can set up video interviews and/or technical tests with the applicants, from our offices, to help you select the best option. If you are not satisfied with any of the applicants we provide, there is no obligation to move forward with us.
Are you like Freelancer site? Do employees work from their homes?
No, we are not like any Freelancer site. You hire dedicated employees through us that work in our Western-managed offices in the Philippines. Aside from saving costs, our established infrastructure, recruitment, and supervision provide you with reliable and professional service compared to working with offshore freelancers that often carry multiple risks.
I haven't heard of you before, but was recommended to speak with you?
Having had offices in the Philippines for several years whilst building and growing our own online businesses, we had numerous clients asking us to help with outsourced staffing. We began by helping with recruitment and had clients' staff working from our premises. From there, the service grew naturally and became much larger than we had originally planned, that is why we set up Top Tech Talent and opened UK offices.

Today we have over 50 clients globally working with our dedicated developers.
How long have you been in business?
We formally setup Top Tech Talent in 2014. However, we have been providing dedicated Top Tech Talent services for the past five years to answer the shortage of skilled IT staff in the UK, US and Australia coupled with the booming tech industry.
Can I visit your offices and meet my Top Tech Talent(s)?
We encourage clients to visit our offices to meet and spend time working with their Top Tech Talent team. We've made sure that our offices are well-equipped and always welcoming to our clients.
Services
Top Tech Talent offers two models
  • Dedicated employee model
  • Dedicated team model
You tell us the skill-set and level of experience you require from the developers and we handle the sourcing, background checks, references and technical assessments to provide you with a shortlist of candidates. You hold the final interviews and technical trial. They work from our supervised offices exclusively for you, working the same office hours as your company. You can save costs of up to 80%, compared to hiring locally.

Our team model works in the same way where we initially hire a team leader. They are stationed together in a dedicated area in our offices where we like to add your company branding to the walls and provide the team with branded T-Shirts. This enables a client to have their own offshore office in the Philippines without cost as to management and set up.
What do your services include?
  • We provide your employee with all the office space and tools required to work for you. This includes:
    • hardware, software, deskspace, internet connection, back-up power generator etc.
  • We deal with all the recruitment and vetting employees you require.
  • You set out your rules and regulations for us and your employee, and we supervise and handle any non-work related issues.
  • We provide your business with dedicated space in our offshore offices within our prime offices.
I want to use your service, how do I get started?
You simply provide your dedicated UK Account Manager with the job specifications of the required employee. This is relayed to our HR department who start the selection process internally and externally. We then conduct all first interviews and testing of the candidates and provide you with a shortlist for you to select for final interviews.
I have a unique requirement, will you have the skill set?
We have two established offices in the Philippines, both located in the major technology working districts, which enables us to attract the best talent available. With so many large Western corporations such as Microsoft, Google, and IBM having been out here for many years, there is a pool of experienced talent able to cover most requirements within IT.
Since the employee is an extension of my company, do I need to worry about Payroll Taxes and HR issues?
No, we take care of all of the non-work related issues such as local licensing, permits, and taxes. We handle all human resource management of your Top Tech Talent where we are there to implement both incentives and disciplinaries at your request.
What is a Dedicated Top Tech Talent?
Our service provides dedicated Top Tech Talents that work directly underneath the client. We do not typically take on ad-hoc project work. Each employee you hire works exclusively for you on no other projects. The Top Tech Talent is part of your team just as if they were sat next to you in your office. Our biometric ‘clock in’ system reports ensure your staff member is at their desk working with you each day.

Many clients have started with one employee. However, as their businesses grew, they also grew their teams with Top Tech Talent. A team typically consists of a lead developer to manage the other staff. You communicate with your Lead Developer just as if they were leading the team in your local office they then control, lead, and manage your team of developers. Savings become even more significant when you scale your Top Tech Talent team.
Skills Sets
Which Skill Sets can you provide?
Top Tech Talent can provide you employees with a range of computer programming languages. We have developed an enormous staffing database and aside from PHP, .NET, Ruby on Rails, Python, and iOS, we can provide book-keeping, PAs, customer support staff, too.
How many Top Tech Talents are working at TopTechTalent.co.uk?
We currently have over 80 Top Tech Talents working in our offices with a number of Clients across the UK, US, and Australia. We work with businesses of all sizes including sole traders, SMEs, and Blue Chip companies.
Are all Top Tech Talents proficient in English?
Absolutely. The Philippines is the 3rd largest English speaking nation in the world. This is largely due to overwhelming Western influence since the 1900's. All of our staff hold university degrees and typically take English modules to further their language skills.

The Philippines outsourced industry is now growing at a much faster rate, compared to India, due to the language capabilities and culture synergy.
Can Top Tech Talent provide me with highly skilled and technical employees?
Yes. We have two offices based in the major cities and technical hubs of the Philippines: Manila and Cebu. We have access to some of the most talented developers with skill-sets in the country. We advise discussing specifics with one of our Account Managers who will help you get the Top Tech Talent you need.
I require an employee who needs to be able to speak directly with my clients. Will accents be a problem?
No, you will find all Top Tech Talents are proficient in English. They represent your business and will never mention Top Tech Talent.
